A Life Remembered

Obituary

John William Smith passed away peacefully on March 28, 2026, surrounded by his loving family. Born in Springfield on March 12, 1945, John was a devoted husband, father, and grandfather who touched countless lives with his warmth, humor, and generosity.

A proud veteran and dedicated community member, John spent over 30 years as a teacher, inspiring generations of students to pursue their dreams. He found joy in tending his garden, fishing at Lake Harmony, and Sunday dinners with the whole family gathered around his table.

He is survived by his wife of 52 years, Margaret; children Sarah (David) and Michael (Laura); grandchildren Emma, James, and Lily; and his brother Robert. He was preceded in death by his parents, William and Dorothy Smith.
